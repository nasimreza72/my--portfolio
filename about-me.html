About myself


My name is Nasim Reza born and raised in Bangladesh. I started off my studies in my home country but I knew I had to move to have a better life. At the age of 19 I moved to Berlin. I had to make a living so I stopped my studies and started working. I found my way in the kitchen and ended up as a chef. As much as I love cooking, I always regretted terminating my studies. After 9 years living in Berlin I decided to follow my childhood dream, so I started studying web development and I never looked back!

When I’m not traveling and resting I spend my days experimenting with coding while searching more and more information to become a better web developer everyday. As they say, you never know enough! I’m curious, I search perfection and most of all I’m patient so I always end up with great results. I build websites that give life to any primitive ideas!
